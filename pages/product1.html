Microsoft Surface Laptop

The Microsoft Surface Laptop is a sleek and stylish laptop designed to deliver exceptional performance and portability. With its premium build quality, vibrant display, and impressive battery life, it's perfect for both work and play. 🌟

Key Features

  • Design: Elegant, slim, and lightweight 🌈🖤
  • Display: 13.5-inch PixelSense™ display for vibrant visuals 🌟
  • Performance: Powered by 10th Gen Intel® Core™ i5 or i7 processors 🚀💨
  • Storage: Up to 1TB SSD for fast and reliable storage 💾⚡
  • RAM: 8GB or 16GB RAM options to ensure smooth multitasking 🧠📈
  • Battery Life: Up to 11.5 hours of battery life 🔋⏳
  • Keyboard: Spacious, backlit, and quiet typing experience ⌨️✨
  • Operating System: Windows 11, optimized for productivity 🖥️🎯
  • Colors: Available in multiple colors like Platinum, Matte Black, Sandstone, and Ice Blue 🎨🌟
  • Ports: 1 USB-C, 1 USB-A, 3.5mm headphone jack, Surface Connect port 🎧🔌

Specifications

  • Processor: Intel® Core™ i5 (10th Gen) / Intel® Core™ i7 (10th Gen)
  • Display: 13.5-inch PixelSense™ Display, 2256 x 1504 resolution (201 PPI), 3:2 aspect ratio
  • Graphics: Intel® UHD Graphics (i5) / Intel® Iris™ Plus Graphics (i7)
  • Storage: 128GB, 256GB, 512GB, or 1TB SSD
  • RAM: 8GB or 16GB LPDDR4x RAM
  • Camera: 720p HD camera with Windows Hello facial recognition 📸✨
  • Audio: Omnisonic speakers with Dolby Audio™ 🎶🔊
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.0 🌐📡
  • Dimensions: 12.1 x 8.8 x 0.57 inches (307.8 x 223.3 x 14.48 mm)
  • Weight: 2.79 lbs (1.26 kg) 💼
